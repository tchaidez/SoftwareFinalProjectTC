Welcome to NBAComparison!

With this application, you will be able to compare current ***STARTING*** NBA players, based on 5 career statistical categories:

Points

Rebounds

Assists

Steals

Blocks

In this comparison, the player with the higher stat will have their box be highlighted green. This while indicate who is better in what category. ***Note that just because a player has all zeros for stats doesn't mean that player is considered a bad player, more likely that player is just a rookie and hasn't played any NBA games yet, since it is still pre-season***
